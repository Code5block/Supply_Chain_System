# Supply Chain Management System (SCM)

## 📦 Project Overview

The **Supply Chain Management System (SCM)** is a full-stack web application designed to manage and optimize the complete supply chain process, including inventory management, supplier handling, order processing, product tracking, and delivery management.

The system helps businesses improve operational efficiency by providing a centralized platform for managing suppliers, warehouses, products, customers, and logistics.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🔐 Authentication & Authorization
- User registration and login
- Secure authentication system
- Role-based access control (Admin, Supplier, Manager, Customer)

### 📊 Dashboard
- Real-time supply chain overview
- Inventory statistics
- Order tracking
- Business performance insights

### 📦 Product Management
- Add, update, delete products
- Product categorization
- Stock availability tracking
- Product search and filtering

### 🏭 Supplier Management
- Manage supplier information
- Supplier performance tracking
- Supplier-product relationship management

### 🏬 Inventory Management
- Monitor stock levels
- Warehouse management
- Low-stock alerts
- Inventory updates

### 🛒 Order Management
- Create and manage orders
- Order status tracking
- Purchase and sales order handling

### 🚚 Logistics Management
- Shipment tracking
- Delivery status updates
- Transportation management

### 📈 Reports & Analytics
- Sales reports
- Inventory reports
- Supply chain analytics
- Data visualization
